HOW IT WORKS

THE CLOSER teaches real sales the way you actually learn it, by doing the work, learning a move, then using it on a real person. No lectures and no quizzes. The lesson is the gameplay.

1
Recon
Walk the town and talk to people to uncover the real problem under what the client actually says.
2
Sales University
Learn the move for this client and pass a quick practice rep to equip it for the pitch.
3
The Pitch
Use the move in conversation against a rapport meter. Read the buyer instead of pushing them.
4
The Job
Close the sale, then do the work in a quick arcade round for bonus points.
You win by understanding and serving the buyer, never by pressure or hype. Manipulation is built to lose.
Move with the arrows, WASD, or the on screen pad. Press A, Enter, or Space to act. Your lifetime points and progress save automatically.

Objective
